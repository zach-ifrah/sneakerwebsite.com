About - Who am I & What is This Website?

Hello! I am a secondary 3 student at St. Thomas High School in the IB program and working in a design coding class.

This project forced students to build a website using code: HTML and CSS particularly. While selecting a topic to make my own website, I chose something that greatly interests me - sneakers. This website has brought you to you great information upon this topic such as the history of sneakers, the value of the sneaker market and more.

Finally, I would like to give a huge thank you to my incredible design teacher who taught me as well as many others what we know. He guided us through amazing tutorials and fantastic assignments to build our skills. Thank you, Mr. Katz.

With that, I hope you enjoy my website.
